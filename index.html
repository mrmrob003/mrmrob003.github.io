About me

I am a theoretical physicist, primarily interested in exploring the remarkable connections between quantum field theory and geometry. My research focuses on modern geometric approaches to scattering amplitudes, including on-shell methods, positive geometry, tropical geometry, cluster algebras, etc. I am currently completing my PhD in Physics at the University of Hertfordshire under the supervision of Dr Tomasz Łukowski.

Outside of research, I enjoy playing speed chess, solving killer sudoku, and adventuring with my beautiful wife.
